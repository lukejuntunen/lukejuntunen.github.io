Exquisite Corpse



2012 - Single Channel Video
Collaboration with Stephanie Manzi, Gina Amico, Patrick Fullmer, Marie London, and Elana Korn

Exquisite Corpse attempts to decontextualize the surrealist practice of the same name into a video format. The first portion of the piece was shot by myself and then the last 15 seconds of my filming were shown to the next participant and so on. The smei-blind nature towards what was filmed previously as well as the variety of experience between the participants allows for a simultaneously connected and disjointed experience that falls inline with the surrealist work it emulates.

Moving Shadow



2013 - Projected Single Channel Video Installation

Moving Shadows represents my exploration into space and light through the use of projected image. It utilizes the projector not as a means to display video, but as a source of light an shadow. The projector casts the shadow of objects that are not physically existant in the space to create a sort of phantom room that only exists in shadows cast on the ground, allowing the two dimensional video to create sculptural space in an otherwise and in actuality empty area.

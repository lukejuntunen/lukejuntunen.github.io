Tell Us Something We

Don't Care About



2013 - Public Intervention - Purchase College, Purchase, NY
Collaboration with Reinaldo Romero and Carolina Melo

For this public intervention, the three of us posed as an outside organization visiting the college to collect information. When students came up, we then asked them to tell us something that we don't care about. The project attempts to not only create an absurd situation in a public space, but also allows the students to give a voice to some lesser known facts or ideas whether about themselves or just in general.

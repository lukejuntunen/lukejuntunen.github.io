About



Hi, I'm Luke Juntunen and I'm a New Media artist who makes stuff in Rochester, NY.

I enjoy exploring the what digital media is and can be in the context of artistic and design practice. I seek to push the boundaries of what the medium I work with can do and can be considered as and also how it can fit into a historical view of art and design as well as it's medium.

What is new media? - New Media is a multidisciplinary category covering most things digital, that is Web Design, Programming, Video Production, Sound design, Installation art and sculpture, and a lot more.

OUR FOUNDER


Luke Juntunen is a graduating New Media senior at Purchase College. In his high school years, he was diagnosed with Type 1 Diabetes, drastically changing the course of his life and eventually inspiring the name of the collective.. In college, he has studied the necessary skills to create creative ways to aid himself and others in their struggles against illness and disease through gaming.

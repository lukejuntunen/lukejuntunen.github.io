Earthrise



2013 - Single Channel Motion Graphics Video

Earthrise attempts to recreate the same off-putting single frame effect used in my previous video This is Art whilst utilizing a wider visual vocabulary through the use of images and video within the composition. Once again, the video creates a ghost in the machine that breaks through the video and effectively breaks the diegesis of the video's world as a faux b-movie intro sequence.
